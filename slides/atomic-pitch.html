The Solution
Your budget
builds your
world.
Atomic turns daily finance habits into a game you actually want to come back to.
🏠

Level Up Your Room

Hit budget goals → unlock furniture. Your room literally reflects your habits.

📋

Daily & Weekly Quests

Small actions that build lasting habits. View expenses, stay on goal, read your tip.

Auto-sync actual transactions — no manual entry, always accurate.

AI Finance Tips (OpenAI)

Personalized daily insights based on your real spending patterns.
